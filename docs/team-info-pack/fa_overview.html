Product Overview — The Challenge
02
The Challenge
Every weekend, thousands of grassroots matches across the UK face the same problem: finding a qualified referee. County FAs have no centralised system to connect clubs with officials, leaving coaches to coordinate through WhatsApp groups, phone trees, and word of mouth.
73%
of coaches cite referee availability as their single biggest operational challenge
5+ hrs
per week wasted coordinating officials via calls, texts, and messages
30%
of grassroots fixtures impacted by late cancellations or no-shows

The FA’s grassroots network covers over 40,000 clubs, 130,000 teams, and nearly 30,000 registered referees. Yet the process of allocating officials to fixtures remains manual, fragmented, and inconsistent across counties. The result: overworked volunteers, unfilled fixtures, and a referee retention crisis.

  • No central platform for coaches to search referee availability in real time
  • Referees have no visibility of local fixtures needing coverage
  • County FAs lack data on booking patterns, referee workload, and coverage gaps
  • Last-minute cancellations cascade — one no-show can affect multiple fixtures
  • No digital audit trail for bookings, payments, or safeguarding compliance
  • Referee recruitment and retention suffers without modern tooling

The Solution

Whistle Connect gives County FAs a single platform to connect every coach with every available referee. Clubs post fixtures, referees set availability, and the system handles matching, offers, confirmations, and messaging — all with built-in FA verification, DBS tracking, and safeguarding compliance. One dashboard replaces the entire coordination chain.

One dashboard. Every match. Every referee. Updated in seconds, not hours. Fully auditable. FA compliant.

Product Overview — Verification
07
FA Verified. Always Compliant.

Every referee on Whistle Connect is verified through the County FA. Our admin verification queue ensures that FA numbers, DBS certificates, and safeguarding qualifications are checked and monitored — giving coaches confidence that every official is qualified.

FA VERIFIED
Qualified • Cleared • Compliant
Every verified referee displays the FA Verified badge on their profile, visible to all coaches during search. Unverified referees are flagged and cannot receive booking offers until cleared.

Verification Areas

FA Number Check

Every referee submits their official FA registration number during sign-up. Administrators cross-reference with County FA records before activation.

DBS Tracking

Enhanced DBS clearance status is recorded and monitored. Automated expiry reminders alert administrators before certificates lapse.

Safeguarding

Safeguarding course completion is tracked as part of the verification process. Required for all youth football officiating roles.

Admin Queue

Two-stage verification queue: Awaiting Response and Resolved. One-click approve or reject with automated email notifications to referees.
